# 🌍 AI for Good Hackathon

Welcome to the **AI for Good Hackathon** by **Future Tech Kid** 🚀  
This hackathon brings together talented developers, builders, and problem-solvers to create meaningful AI-powered solutions for real-world challenges.

Your goal is not just to build something impressive, but to build something **useful, responsible, clear, and impactful**.

# ⏰ 11:59 PM GMT

## 🎯 Hackathon Theme

## AI for Good

Your project should use AI to address a real problem that affects people, communities, businesses, education, health, sustainability, accessibility, livelihoods, or society at large.

We encourage you to think beyond the technology itself:

- Who is this helping?
- Why does this problem matter?
- Is AI truly needed?
- Could this become something sustainable after the hackathon?
- Can your team explain it clearly to people without a technical background?

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ What the Jury Is Looking For

The jury will evaluate every team through five main lenses:

| Criteria | What It Means |
|---|---|
| 🎯 **Real-World Relevance** | Does your solution address a genuine, well-understood problem? |
| 🤖 **AI Execution** | Is AI used meaningfully, not just as a buzzword? |
| 💰 **Sustainability & Income Potential** | Could this become a real venture, product, service, or livelihood opportunity? |
| 🗣️ **Presentation & Clarity** | Can you explain your solution clearly in 5 minutes to a non-technical audience? |
| 🤝 **Team Collaboration** | Did the team build it together, and can everyone defend the solution? |

## 📌 Required README To-Dos for Each Team

Each team must include the following sections in their own project `README.md` file before submitting.

### ✅ TODO 1: Problem Statement

Explain the real-world problem your team is solving.

Your answer should include:

- What is the problem?
- Who is affected by it?
- Why does it matter?
- What happens if this problem is not solved?

---

### ✅ TODO 2: Proposed Solution

Describe your solution in a simple and clear way.

Please include:

- What your product or prototype does
- How users interact with it
- What makes it useful
- What makes it different from existing solutions

---

### ✅ TODO 3: How AI Is Used

Explain clearly where and how AI is part of your solution.

Be specific about:

- What AI model, tool, or technique you used
- What task the AI performs
- Why AI is needed for this solution
- How the AI improves the user experience or outcome

The jury wants to see that AI is used meaningfully — not just mentioned as a buzzword.

---
